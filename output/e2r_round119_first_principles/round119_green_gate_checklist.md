# Round-119 Stage 3-Green Gate Checklist

| gate | effect | pass example | fail example |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `cross_evidence` | block_green_if_missing | disclosure plus financial_actual plus research_report | single news headline or search result |
| `eps_fcf_durability` | block_green_if_missing | FY1/FY2 OP and FCF revisions supported by backlog or recurring demand | temporary demand spike |
| `structural_visibility` | block_green_if_weak | multi-year contract or export channel sell-through | theme keyword with no visibility field |
| `old_frame_mispricing` | downgrade_to_watch_or_4b_if_saturated | cyclical discount remains while earnings path changed | new frame already universally accepted |
| `price_path_alignment` | block_green_or_mark_price_only | price rerates after evidence date without breaking below stage price materially | price ran first and evidence never arrived |
| `no_hard_redteam` | hard_block_green | no filing delay, no auditor resignation, no cash runway collapse | auditor resignation or contract cancellation |
| `not_saturated_4b` | mark_4b_watch_or_elevated | revision still strong and valuation runway remains | target multiples saturated with crowded reports |

## Easy Example

A company can have a strong transformer headline and a price breakout, but if there is no contract amount, no margin evidence, no EPS/FCF revision, and no price-path validation after the dated evidence, it stays below Stage 3-Green.
